01 — Artist

Ada Fen · 2026

Sound that lives
in the in-between.

Ada Fen makes electronic pop music that doesn't resolve cleanly. Warm synths underneath cold arrangements. Hooks you can't shake. Lyrics that land differently the third time you hear them.

Ada Fen is a solo electronic project built around the friction between intimacy and distance — the emotional register that synthwave opened up in the '80s, recontextualized through a contemporary pop lens. Her productions layer processed vocals against clean analog-style basslines, with melodies that feel nostalgic for places that never existed. Equal parts neon boulevard and 3am apartment window.

Based on the East Coast. Records late. Ships clean.
