Section 03
Failure Modes
Where can this go wrong? Select all that apply to your feature.
🧠 Hallucination
Model outputs confident but factually wrong content
🪤 Over-reliance
User stops thinking critically and defers to AI blindly
⚖️ Bias / fairness
Output reflects training biases that harm certain groups
🔐 Privacy leak
Sensitive context leaks into prompts, outputs, or logs
⚡ Irreversible action
AI-triggered action that's hard or impossible to undo
🌫️ Scope ambiguity
User doesn't know what the AI will or won't do
🐢 Latency / timeout
Slow responses break the workflow or imply failure
💔 Trust collapse
A single bad output poisons perception of the whole feature
