Where to show up. How to show up there.

@adafenmusic
4–5×/week Grid + Reels + Stories

  • Grid: moodboard + art direction stills — 1–2×/week
  • Stories: daily-ish behind the scenes, polls, Q&A
  • Carousels: track breakdowns + text storytelling — 1×/week
  • Link in bio: always points to latest release/Bandcamp

@adafenmusic

  • "How I made this" — 30s process breakdown — 1×/week
